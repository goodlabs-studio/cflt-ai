**HYG-01 — wiki-stats.py (lines 57, 59, 75):**
Replace the three lines containing invalid Unicode em-dash syntax:

Line 57: change `print(f"\n{─*50}")` to `print(f"\n{'-' * 50}")`
Line 59: change `print(f"{─*50}")` to `print(f"{'-' * 50}")`
Line 75: change `print(f"{─*50}\n")` to `print(f"{'-' * 50}\n")`

Do NOT change anything else in the file. The fix is replacing the Unicode box-drawing character `─` (U+2500) with the ASCII hyphen `'-'` inside the f-string multiplication expression.

**HYG-02 — wiki-lint.py (line 81):**
Replace the broken-link regex on line 81:

Before: `links = re.findall(r"\[.*?\]\((wiki/[^)]+)\)", content)`
After:  `links = re.findall(r"\[.*?\]\((wiki/[^)#]+(?:#[^)]*)?)\)", content)`

This change makes the regex correctly capture optional fragment identifiers (#section) in wiki-internal links. The outer capture group now matches `wiki/path.md` or `wiki/path.md#anchor`.

**HYG-03 — evaluate.md (lines 21-22):**
