## Overview

Six planned phases delivered the Confluent operational and knowledge agent for FSI engagements. Phase 0 fixed foundational hygiene and established the canon overlay stack and MANIFEST.yaml contract. Phase 1 unified the knowledge skill with a tested triage path. Phase 2 hardened the review skill with reproducible claim extraction and customer-ready output. Phases 3a through 3c built the act rail incrementally — read-only plan, human-gated apply, then full per-profile tool classification. Phases F.1, G.1, and G.2c added post-roadmap extensions discovered during real engagement use: FRANZ pre-confirmed apply integration, terraform-module executor for /dsp-apply Step 7, and the mcp-confluent tool-classification rename to align with the live 1.3.0 registry.
